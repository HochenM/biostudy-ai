Problem

Students struggle with scattered and unstructured learning resources across lectures, textbooks, and online materials, making it difficult to focus on what is truly important for exam preparation.

Solution

BioStudy AI is an AI-powered learning assistant that organizes educational content into structured explanations and helps students learn in a focused, course-aligned way for better understanding and exam preparation.

Users

High school and university students studying structured academic subjects such as biology, mathematics, and computer science.

Features
Explains topics in a structured teaching format
Solves problems step-by-step
Generates study roadmaps for subjects
Answers student questions based on course-relevant knowledge
Helps with exam preparation and revision
MVP Scope

A simple AI tutor system that supports Q&A, step-by-step explanations, and study planning. No mobile app, user accounts, or advanced integrations in the initial version.
